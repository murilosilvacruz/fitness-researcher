**PROMPT FOR VEO (GOOGLE) - VIRAL REEL GENERATION**

---

Create a vertical 9:16 video reel (8-15 seconds) for Instagram targeting Brazilian women aged 40–55. The video should educate about how exercise modulates progesterone and affects the menstrual cycle.

**COLOR PALETTE:** Terracotta #C9614A, Sage #7B9E87, Off-white #F7F5F2

**STRUCTURE & VIRAL TECHNIQUES:**

**HOOK (0–3s) — OPENING HOOK + SPEED RAMP:**
Start with a fast-paced visual of a woman (Afro-Brazilian or Parda/Mestiça, bronze-warm skin, natural or wavy hair, 40–50 years old) in motion during exercise — running on an urban park path or doing strength training in a bright apartment. Quick camera movement; text overlay appears: "Seu exercício está mexendo com seus hormônios" (Your exercise is messing with your hormones). Duration: 2.5s. Immediate visual contrast between static moment and dynamic action.

**HOLD (3–12s) — SLOW-MO STRATEGIC + SPLIT SCREEN + FACE HOLD:**
Cut to slow-motion shot of the same woman's face (FACE HOLD: 4–6s static camera, close-up on eyes/expression) showing moment of realization/awareness. Simultaneously, SPLIT SCREEN on right side shows animated or graphic visualization of progesterone molecule fluctuating in response to exercise type (strength vs. cardio). Text appears in Sage green: "Progesterona sobe, desce — depende do seu treino" (Progesterone rises, falls — depends on your workout). Camera pacing: cut every 2–3 seconds between face close-up and hormone visualization. Include 1-2 second micro-transition showing her hand on chest (intimate POV gesture).

**PAYOFF (últimos 3–5s) — BEFORE/AFTER + LOOP PERFECT:**
Quick BEFORE/AFTER split: left side shows woman tired/fatigued (desaturated, slower movement), right side shows energized woman (saturated Terracotta-Sage tones, quick movement, smile). Final frame: woman in bright apartment or park, looking directly at camera with knowing expression. Text: "Salva esse post — você vai entender seu corpo" (Save this post — you'll understand your body). Camera loops subtly back to opening motion, creating perfect loop effect.

**TECHNICAL DETAILS:**
- Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio
- Brazilian setting: urban park with morning light OR modern apartment with natural window light
- Typography: clean sans-serif in Off-white on Sage/Terracota backgrounds
- Transitions: quick cuts (0.5–1s), minimal motion blur
- Audio: upbeat, educational tone; subtle ambient + voiceover in Portuguese (optional)
- No text larger than 40% of frame
- Closed captions in Portuguese

**TALENT BRIEF:**
Feature one woman, Afro-Brazilian or Parda (warm brown skin tone, natural/wavy hair texture, age 42–50). Authentic, relatable expression; show micro-expressions of realization and confidence.
